A kitchen for an ornate Victorian room where the existing ceiling
plaster and tall sash window had to be respected. The cabinetry sits
quietly against the wall: deep shaker doors painted in a warm white,
full-height larders to either side of the integrated ovens.

The island is the lone bold gesture — a marble top with a moulded
shaker base, lit by two brass globe pendants hung from the rose. Antique
brass taps and a brushed brass bridge sit over a butler sink set into
the marble.

Designed to look like it could have always been there.
