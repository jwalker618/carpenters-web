A kitchen built around a single, confident colour. The island and tall
units are sprayed in a deep forest green; the worktop is a single piece
of white quartz, kept deliberately plain so the cabinetry can sing.

Oak veneer lines the open shelving and the small drinks niche above the
oven, picking up the warmth of the wide-plank floor and softening all
the painted surfaces around it.

Subtle brass details throughout — pulls, taps, kick — tie the room
together without competing with the colour.
