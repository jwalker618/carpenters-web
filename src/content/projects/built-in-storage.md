A series of fitted-storage pieces commissioned together for a single
house. Each is shaped to its space — angled to follow a sloping ceiling
under the eaves, dropped into chimney alcoves, or pressed flat against
a wall under the stairs.

The doors are kept plain and full-height so the joinery reads as
architecture rather than furniture, with hardwood pulls routed in to
keep the faces uninterrupted.

Inside, every wardrobe is set up for the way the owner actually uses
it: long-hang, short-hang, soft-close drawers, integrated lighting.
