A built-in cabinet for a client who needed storage that looked like
furniture, not joinery. The carcass is solid white oak with quarter-sawn
panels chosen for their straight, calm grain.

The lower doors hide everyday clutter; the upper shelves are fully
adjustable on a discreet pin system so the owner can rearrange books and
objects over time. Hardware is solid brass, left unlacquered so it will
develop a patina with use.

Finished with a low-sheen water-based lacquer for durability.
