A bathroom vanity designed to feel weighty and quiet at the same time.
The worktop is a single cast concrete slab with two recessed basins,
sitting on a wall-hung carcass painted off-white.

Behind the white drawer fronts the interiors are solid walnut — a small
private extravagance that reveals itself every time a drawer opens.
Brushed nickel taps and wall-mounted spouts keep the surface uncluttered.
